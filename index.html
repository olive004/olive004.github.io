Welcome

I am a researcher specializing in synthetic biology, machine learning, and computational biology. My work focuses on developing innovative tools and methods at the intersection of biology and artificial intelligence.

About Me

Currently pursuing a DPhil, I work on projects ranging from evolutionary landscape analysis to genetic circuit design. My research applies computational approaches to biological systems to advance our understanding of complex biological processes.

I'm passionate about open science, reproducible research, and creating tools that enable other researchers to push the boundaries of what's possible in computational biology.

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning for Biological Systems
  • Evolutionary Dynamics & Fitness Landscapes
  • RNA Design & Engineering
